# Meridian SaaS Co. Loan Agreement Excerpt

## Section 4.1 Minimum Liquidity / Cash Runway
Borrower shall maintain at least $8.0 million of unrestricted cash and at least 4.0 months of cash runway, calculated as unrestricted cash divided by latest monthly gross burn.

## Section 4.2 ARR Growth Floor
Borrower shall maintain trailing twelve-month ARR growth of at least 20.0% year over year, measured against the same calendar month in the prior year.

## Section 4.3 Net Burn Multiple Cap
Borrower shall maintain a net burn multiple no greater than 1.50x, calculated as quarterly gross burn divided by quarterly net new ARR. If quarterly net new ARR is zero or negative, the covenant is deemed breached.

## Section 4.4 Net Revenue Retention Floor
Borrower shall maintain net revenue retention of at least 100.0% for each reporting month.

## Section 4.5 Committed MRR Interpretation
Committed MRR may include signed customer expansions scheduled to begin within 60 days. Management's December 2026 pilot expansion is verbally approved but not countersigned, creating an ambiguous edge case requiring human review rather than automatic inclusion.
